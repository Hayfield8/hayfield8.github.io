Projects

CVS Tracker 

For my first project I wanted to make something that mattered to me and that I would be a user of. As a sufferer of a rare condition known as cyclical vomiting syndrome, a condition that causes repeated and prolonged episodes of vomiting that have many triggers, it can be hard to find resources out there to help. Due to the nature of the syndrome it is difficult to keep track of what may cause the vomiting so I felt it would be useful to record each episode in order to look for any common or recurring patterns. To make this possible I had to make sure that as much information about each vomiting episode can be recorded and accessed at a later date. This program does exactly that, it allows me to record every time I suffer an episode along with important information about what I have eaten, my mood, sleep level etc. From use of the CVS Tracker so far, I have realised that the MSG in Chinese takeaways is a sure trigger for my episodes. This is still a work in progress and I hope to add more useful features over time. If you or anyone you know is a sufferer of CVS I hope that the CVS Tracker will help with your management of the symptoms, I’m always looking for feedback so feel free to contact me with any you may have!
